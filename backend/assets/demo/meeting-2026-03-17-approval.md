## 2. CEO Questions & Discussion

**Michael:** "What's the expected ROI on the AI infrastructure investment?"

**Tony:** Based on our projections:
- Customer acquisition cost reduction: 25% (AI-powered onboarding)
- Support ticket reduction: 40% (AI knowledge base)
- Engineering productivity: 30% increase (AI-assisted development)
- Estimated annual value: **$800K-1.2M** against $312K investment = **2.5-3.8x ROI**

**Michael:** "How does our AI spend compare to competitors?"

**Sarah:** Industry benchmark for AI-native SaaS companies is 8-12% of engineering budget on AI infrastructure. Our 7% is slightly below average, which is appropriate for our stage. We can scale up in FY2027 if ROI is proven.

**Michael:** "What happens if we don't hit hiring targets?"

**Rachel:** Contingency plan:
1. Engage contract ML engineers (2-3 month bridge, ~$200/hr)
2. Reallocate internal engineers for cross-training
3. Delay Q3-Q4 features rather than compromise quality

## 3. Board Decision

**Michael approved the FY2026 engineering budget of $4,425,000** with the following conditions:
1. Quarterly budget reviews with variance analysis
2. AI ROI metrics dashboard by end of Q1
3. Hiring progress report monthly to leadership
4. Vendor renegotiation results by end of Q1
