### Q5: How are board members selected? Must they be parents?

**Status**: PASS | **Time**: 17.1s

Based on the provided documents, board members are selected through a combination of election and appointment processes. While parents make up the membership of the Association that elects some members, not all Board members are required to be parents.

### Selection Methods

There are two primary ways Board members are selected:

1.  **Election by the Association:**
    *   Seven members of the Board are elected by the Association of Parents [8].
    *   Candidates for election must be recommended by two Association members and submit an application in writing at least four weeks in advance of the election date [8].
    *   Candidates receiving the largest number of votes are deemed elected [8].

2.  **Appointment by the Board:**
    *   Five to seven Board members are appointed by the Board itself [8].
    *   When a vacancy occurs (e.g., for an appointed position), the Board notifies the community and calls for nominations. A "Candidate Vetting Group" or "Parent Advisory Group" (comprised of 2 Board members and 6-8 parents) reviews candidates and passes a list to the Board for the final decision [3].

3.  **Consul General:**
    *   The U.S. Consul General resident in Shanghai, or their designated representative, serves as a member of the Board [8].

### Requirement to be a Parent

The requirement to be a parent (a member of the Association) depends on how the Board member is selected:

*   **Elected Members:** Must be members of the Association. Since the Association is composed of the parents and legal guardians of children registered at the School, elected Board members must be parents [8].
*   **Appointed Members:** Do **not** necessarily have to be members of the Association (i.e., they do not have to be parents) [8].
*   **Consul General:** Serves as a member by virtue of their office, not necessarily because they are a parent [8].

### Eligibility Restrictions
Even if a candidate is a parent, they are ineligible for Board membership if they are:
*   A paid employee of the School, or an immediate family member of a paid school employee [8].
*   The spouse of a current Board member [8].

*   **Design for Well-Being (设计福祉)：** 承认自然的重要性，设计融合自然材料、光线和景观的空间，以促进身心健康。

根据 **5.30 Salary Deductions（薪资扣除）** [1][2]：
*   学校承诺不进行不当扣薪，并遵守合同约定。
*   通常情况下，任何薪资扣款都需要员工的书面授权。
*   如果发生扣款错误，学校将进行调查并全额退还员工。

### 6. 休假政策
各类外籍员工的休假类型（如病假、个人假、丧假、产假/陪产假等）详情列在 **5.27P Compensation and Benefits Procedures** 中 [1][2]。具体更新包括：
*   **丧假：** 更新为“Compassionate Leave”（同情假），外籍员工若直系亲属（父母、配偶、子女、兄弟姐妹）去世，可享有 **14 个连续日历日** 的假期 [2]。
*   **产假/陪产假：** 女性员工享有 98 天产假（含 15 天产前假和 83 天产后假）；男性员工享有 10 天陪产假。此外还包括收养假等规定 [2]。

### 7. 外籍员工福利
针对外籍员工的特定福利（如住房、保险等）详情包含在 **5.31 Benefits For Expatriate Employees** 及其程序中 [1][2]。
