### Q2. Are there any Board policies for student travel?

**Status**: ✅ PASS | **Time**: 36.7s | **Matched**: field trip, excursion, activity travel

Based on the provided documents, there are Board policies and procedures related to student travel, specifically concerning **Field Trips and Excursions** and **Activity Travel**.

**Field Trips and Excursions**
The Board recognizes the value of out-of-classroom educational experiences and supports them provided they meet safety standards, offer equitable support and opportunity among similar student groups, and enhance or strengthen curriculum objectives. The primary purpose of these trips must be to further the educational goals of the school. Students may be required to pay all or a portion of the trip costs [2].

Specific procedures for these trips include:
*   **Approval:** The Principal must approve school-sponsored trips based on security, safety, and educational value.
*   **Documentation:** A parental authorization form and liability waiver must be on file prior to participation.
*   **Chaperones:** Unless special circumstances apply, the target ratio is 10 students per 1 adult with a minimum of 2 adults. Trips outside Shanghai or in areas with limited medical services require a nurse.
*   **Departure and Release:** All students must leave from school to attend field trips. Guardians may take students home from the site provided it does not miss instruction time and the school is notified. Students may only leave with non-guardians with advance written authorization [2].

**Activity Travel**
For school-sponsored activity trips that are approved by the administration for funding or identified in the annual budget, the school provides a partial subsidy for travel expenses. The remaining balance must be paid by the student prior to travel [1].

*   **紧急储备基金：** 学校建立并维护紧急储备基金，目标是使该基金最终不低于年度总运营费用的25%。董事会负责确保在该基金余额低于25%时调整收入或减少开支，直到储备金恢复到该水平 [3]。

**4. 董事会治理与委员会运作**
*   董事会通过委员会（如财务委员会）进行工作，委员会成员由主席任命，任期通常为2年，且可连任。委员会负责在特定领域（如财务监督、资产保护）协助董事会履行职责 [1]。
