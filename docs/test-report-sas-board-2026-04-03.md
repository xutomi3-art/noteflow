### Q5. Tell me what the policy says about Board members. Do they have to be parents?

**Status**: ✅ PASS | **Time**: 6.6s | **Matched**: Association of Parents, elected, appointed, need not be, must be a member

Based on the provided documents, here is what the policy states regarding whether Board members must be parents:

**General Requirement**
No, Board members do not universally have to be parents. The requirement to be a parent depends specifically on how the Board member is selected (elected vs. appointed).

*   **Appointed Board Members:** If a person is appointed to fill a vacancy left by an *appointed* member, they "need not be a member of the Association" (which consists of parents/legal guardians) [1].
*   **Elected Board Members:** If a person is selected to replace an *elected* Board member, the selectee "must be a member of the Association of Parents" [1].

**Candidate Vetting Group Composition**
While the Board itself may include non-parents in appointed seats, the policy dictates that the **Candidate Vetting Group** (which reviews candidates for appointed positions) must include parents. This group is composed of two sitting Board members and between 6 and 8 parents selected to be representative of the school community [1].

**Code of Conduct**
All Board members are expected to adhere to a Code of Conduct, which includes avoiding conflicts of interest and maintaining confidentiality [2] [6]. Additionally, Board members are expected to model exemplary behavior and represent the interests of the School as a whole [4].

*   **连任资格**：在延长的任期届满的年份，董事会成员可以申请通过选举或任命开启新的任期 [6]。

总结来说，一名董事会成员的初始任期为2年，通常可以延长2年，在特定情况下还可能再获得1年的额外延长。在此之后，成员仍具备资格申请连任。
